MECH TALKS - Paper Presentation

Date: March 15, 2025

Location: SPMVV Campus

Mech Talks is a technical event in which the participants should prepare their paper and they have to present before the judges.

There is only one round and the best is chosen by the judges.

General Rules and Guidelines for competition:

  • Open to all B.Tech / M.Tech students.
  • A paper can have a maximum of two authors.
  • Papers must be IEEE Format but not exceed 15 pages. (Check the IEEE template)
  • Papers should be sent through E-Mail:mecharena2k25@gmail.com
  • Detail of member(s): Name, Year, Department, Institute, Contact number, email ID, Title should be mentioned in the paper.
  • Fee payment must be done only for Selected abstracts for paper presentation

Themes for competition:

  • DRONE TECHNOLOGY
  • HYBRID VEHICLES
  • GREEN TECHNOLOGIES3D PRINTING
  • ROBOTICS
  • ENVIRONMENTAL ENGINEERING
  • ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE
  • 3D PRINTING
