SPOT YOUR POT - Tech Hunt

Date: 24 & 25 February, 2025

Location: SOET, SPMVV

SPOT YOUR POT is a technical event in which each participant attempts to find out something, that has been hidden, using written directions or clues.

Consists of two rounds, the participant who finds the clues faster will be moved to the next round.

General Rules and Guidelines for competition:

  • Each Participant should be provided with Same Clues.
  • You have to search for clues in allotted a reasonly.
  • You have to find the clue with in time limit
  • No extra time will be Provided.
