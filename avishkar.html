AVISHKAR - Poster Presentation

Date: March 15, 2025

Location: SPMVV Campus

Poster Presentation is a technical event in which the participants should prepare their poster and they have to bring the poster to demonstrate before the judges.

There is only one round and the best is chosen by the judges and the respective prizes are given away.

General Rules and Guidelines for competition:

  • Each team can have maximum of 1 members.
  • The competition is open to all graduate students.
  • Members should have a valid student ID card of their college.
  • A team member can participate in only one posterpresentation.

Themes for competition:

  • DRONE TECHNOLOGY
  • HYBRID VEHICLES
  • GREEN TECHNOLOGIES3D PRINTING
  • ROBOTICS
  • ENVIRONMENTAL ENGINEERING
  • ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE
  • 3D PRINTING
